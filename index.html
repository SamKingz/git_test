AboutPokémon

Pokémon are creatures of all shapes and sizes who live in the wild or alongside humans.

For some people, Pokémon are pets. Others use them for battling. Many people catch and train Pokémon as a hobby. Pokémon are also used for work, such as helping with construction or performing rescues.

Pokemons
are very versatile and can be used in many different ways.
